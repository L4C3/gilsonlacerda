Case Study

Rhema NRC – Brand & Website Redesign

2024 Role: Web Design & Development WordPress / Elementor Brand Integration
Branding UX Web Design

Overview

This project transformed a simple Rhema Parties page into a unified brand experience connecting the NRC (Rhema’s recreation center), party packages, sports fields, and the skating rink. The redesign introduced new visual identity elements, integrated service areas, and improved navigation—making it easier for the community to explore and book events.

Problem → Solution → Impact

  • Problem: The original Rhema Parties page was isolated, with minimal visuals and no clear connection to other NRC services.
  • Solution: A full redesign that tied Rhema Parties, NRC Fields, and Rhema Skating under a cohesive visual and navigational structure.
  • Impact: Increased cross-service visibility, more event bookings, and a stronger community-facing brand presence.

Highlights

  • Integrated branding across Parties, Fields, and Skating.
  • Custom-designed section layouts with Elementor Pro in WordPress.
  • Mobile-friendly design with clear calls-to-action for bookings.
  • Visual hierarchy using bold logos, vibrant colors, and themed typography.
  • Navigation linking all NRC service areas for a unified experience.

  • UX flow & content structure
  • Responsive design testing
